Updated recommendation: G now, F next, H as the big swing

Why Round 1 can't fully win: every same-screen option — even the quietest card — adds a second interactive element next to a primary CTA that is already visually weak (brand-500 pink with dark text). Two objectives on one static screen will always share the user's ~10 seconds. The fix is sequencing, not styling.

G — exit-intent takeover is the cleanest quick win: this screen stays byte-identical to production (zero risk to the booking funnel, an easy sell to the app team), and TV gets a full-screen, zero-competition moment at the exact point the user is a lost lead anyway — on back-press. Must be frequency-capped (once per session) so it never feels naggy.

F — promote on 2nd fail is the smartest targeting: first unserviceable result stays pure; if the user searches another address and that also fails, they've exhausted the primary job — now the TV card earns near-primary treatment with honest copy ("यहाँ भी अभी नहीं…"). Just a session counter in Flutter.

H — notify-me → TV as reward is the biggest product idea: it fixes the screen's real gap (there is no "tell me when Wiom arrives" action today — a lost lead), captures the lead, and then gifts TV on the confirmation moment when goodwill is highest and the primary job is complete. Needs backend, so it's a roadmap item, not this sprint.

E (text footnote) remains the trivial fallback if we must ship something on-screen today. index.html still shows Round 1's A as the on-screen "After" until a direction is locked.
