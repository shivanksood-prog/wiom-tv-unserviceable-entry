Recommendation: A — quiet card in the content zone

On the dark screen the recommendation was "move the card after the actions." On the live light screen that flips: the bottom bar is a small, pinned decision zone — putting the promo there (B) crowds the thumb area and mixes objectives. Meanwhile the content zone has free whitespace right under the address card, and a white/tinted card no longer overpowers a light screen.

A puts a soft brand-tinted card there: message → address → "तब तक व्योम TV देखें — 100% फ्री" with the live-TV mini frame, a chevron instead of a button, and no saturated pink. The eye reads unavailability first, the offer second, and the only real CTA on screen stays the booking button. D's entrance (slide in ~1.5s after first paint) layers on cleanly if we want sequenced attention as polish.

This A design is now the default "After" state in the main prototype (index.html).
